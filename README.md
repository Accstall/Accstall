## 🤔 What Are Softreg, Aged, and PVA Accounts?

**Softreg (Fresh) Accounts** are newly registered accounts ideal for automation software, social media management tools, and bulk operations. They are the most affordable option.

**Aged Accounts** were created months or years ago and carry higher trust scores within platform algorithms. They are preferred for advertising, organic growth strategies, and brand-building.

**PVA (Phone Verified Accounts)** have been verified via SMS, making them more reliable and less likely to face restrictions.
